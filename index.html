ABOUT PARKER IM Core F321

Our brand needs no introduction. PARKER IM writing instruments live up to their name. The PARKER IM collection is a new approach to creativity. With an excellent selection of various high quality finishes, the Parker IM pen will suit almost any look and mood. Comfort, simplicity and high functionality, combined with a modern tapered shape and versatile finish, create a modern Parker style.
